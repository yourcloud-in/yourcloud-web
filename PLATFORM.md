## What is GravRel?

GravRel is India's first **solar-powered**, **DPDP Act 2023 compliant** cloud.
Built for startups, researchers, and enterprises in Tier 2 India.

- 🛡️ Data never leaves India — DPDP compliant from day one
- ☀️ Zero carbon emissions — 100% solar powered
- 💧 Zero water waste — air cooling only
- 💰 60% cheaper than AWS Mumbai
- 📞 CEO personally reachable — ceo@gravrel.com
